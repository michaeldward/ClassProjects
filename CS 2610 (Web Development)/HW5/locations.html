Locations

Alpine

One of our locations is in a beautiful mountainside in a taiga biome. The lodge is based on an alpine lodge and features everything necessary for a wonderful vacation or a comfortable night before you continue your travels.


Castle

Our other location is a wonderful castle in a tundra biome. The castle is based off of Elsa's castle in Disney's Frozen. A castle made entirely out of ice could never exist in real life--that is the beauty of Minecraft!
